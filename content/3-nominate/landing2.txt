Title: Nominate a Project

----

Text: 

Young people's great ideas deserve to be known and shared!

Have you been working on social innovation? Do you think your initiative can be useful for someone else? Tell us about your project!

----

Text2: 

**Requirements**

1) The project must be related to at least one **(link: https://sdsnyouth.org/about#the-sdgs text: SDG)**.

2) The project must have started already. This means:
- Be in test, implementation or growth phase.
- The project has been in operation for more than 5 months.
- The young innovator has a team (+1 people) working with him or her on the project.
- They have been actively seeking for funding (doesn't matter if they got it yet). 

3) The founder or co-founder of the project is aged between 15 and 30 years old. 
OR
More than 51% of the team members are aged 18 to 30 years old.
