Title: About Y20

----

Text: The Youth 20 (or Y20) is the youth engagement group for the G2O, the leading forum of the world's 20 major economies. As the G2O does, each year the Y20 organizes a Summit that reunites youth from all over the G2O territories and guest countries, to debate and work on recommendation that reflect the youth point of views regarding the main topics and priorities chosen by the Presidency. You can read more about the Y2O in (link: http://youth20.org text: their website).
